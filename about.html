Nice People Who Train Hard

Task Karate is a friendly, focused dojo in La Crosse, WI. We build confident movers and helpful training partners through clean mechanics, practical drills, and a community-first mindset.

Meet Randy Thomson

Randy leads Task Karate with a simple recipe: fundamentals, respect, and lots of reps. You’ll find him on the floor demonstrating, correcting, and celebrating progress—one clean technique at a time.
